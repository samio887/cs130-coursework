GirlUp: Northwestern

Promoting Gender Equality

Who We Are

Girl Up NU is a campus chapter of the U.N. Foundation's nonpartisan Girl Up campaign that promotes the education, leadership, and well-being of girls in our community and abroad.

Our Philosophy

Although our organization uses the term "girl," we don't believe that our philosophy or actions should be limited to the gender binary. We welcome and encourage people of all identities to join our organization.

Our Approach

We're committed to fighting for global gender equality, particularly in education, STEM-related fields, and sports, as well as eliminating gender violence worldwide.

What We Do

Advocacy

Efforts for causes you're passionate about in the form of letter-writing on behalf of local and national gender-based legislation and other political events.

Storytelling

Through speaker events with professors and faculty, collaborations with other student orgs, etc!

Fundraising

For local and global Girl Up efforts through merch sales, open mic nights, the annual Global 5k, group yoga classes, etc!
